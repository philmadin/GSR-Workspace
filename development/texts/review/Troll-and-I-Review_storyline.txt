Set in 1958 Scandinavia, the games follows Otto, a young village boy. Out hunting one day, he returns to find his home on fire and his injured mother telling him to flee to safety. Escaping into the wilderness Otto meets a troll, a magical creature believed to be a myth told to children by their parents to keep them in line. They decide to team up to combat the hunters sent to capture the troll, and in turn, save their home.&lt;br&gt;
