<p style="margin-bottom: 0.28cm; line-height: 108%"><i style="margin-bottom: 0.28cm; line-height: 108%">Spec Ops: TheLine</i> is the latest and most probably the last in the long line of the<i style="margin-bottom: 0.28cm; line-height: 108%">Spec Ops</i> series, a group of middling, mostly forgotten militaryshooters that have long since been over shadowed by the likes of Callof Duty and Battlefield. Unlike its predecessors however, <i style="margin-bottom: 0.28cm; line-height: 108%">Spec Ops:The Line</i> goes beyond the bounds of the standard military shooter andasks some very real and very dark questions.</p>
