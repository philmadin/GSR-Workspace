Ventura Bay is a gorgeous city, with wet roads that reflect the light from street lamps and buildings, the city has immense detail for a place that is never physically explored. The city is believable as it is all unique despite the absence of traffic.<br />
<br />
There are a lot of options to customise your vehicle from decals, logos and specific types of paint. After that, there are physical alterations like rims, spoilers and so much more.  It is only limited by imagination and the money you have earned.
