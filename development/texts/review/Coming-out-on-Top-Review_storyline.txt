With Mark no longer hiding his true self from his friends he dives into his final year at College. Mark lives with his friends Penny, a purple haired eccentric developer striving to design a killer app and Ian, attractive, friendly, supportive and adventurous.<br />
<br />
The story follows a fairly straight line however there are a surprising amount of forks in the road for you to choose from, each leading to different plot lines and quite the queue of potential romance options. The amount of replay value here is beyond what can be gleaned from your first few minutes in Mark’s shoes and by the end, you’ll likely find your experience heartwarming, not just uncomfortable/exciting.
