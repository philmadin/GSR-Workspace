A new galaxy, and a whole new host of problems. The fourth entry in the widely adored &lt;i&gt;Mass Effect&lt;/i&gt; series, unfortunately, fails to live up to the greatness of the original trilogy. While introducing vastly improved combat mechanics and a new crew of well-realised characters, &lt;i&gt;Andromeda&lt;/i&gt; is let down by a scourge of bugs and technical issues.
