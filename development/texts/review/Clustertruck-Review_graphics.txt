<i><br>Clustertruck </i>follows in Landfall Game's tradition of simple aesthetics that just work. The game has a distinctive low-poly style which helps reinforce the tongue in cheek nature of the game.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p>While I wouldn't say the game is ever pretty, it is most certainly never ugly. Environments, obstacles and the trucks themselves all stand out with each world being visually distinct and obstacles ranging from trees to lasers are clearly separate from the rest of the game.</p><p><br></p><p>The game runs great as well, though I would expect nothing less. It's a goofy game with a goofy style and it just works.</p>
