For three years, Ethan Winters' girlfriend, Mia, has been missing, presumed dead. One day, Ethan receives an email from her, drawing him to an abandoned house in Louisiana. There, he finds Mia and that's when everything goes wrong.<br><br>For most of the game, you are left in the dark about the events occurring. What little story there is, found in documents scattered around the property. You feel as clueless as Ethan does as he tries to figure out what is wrong with his girlfriend and the Bakers. By the end, you are left unsatisfied with many unanswered questions that may not get resolved until a new game or even DLC.<br>
