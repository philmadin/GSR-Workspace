The visuals of <i>Tokyo Mirage Sessions #FE</i> are some of the best I've seen in a long while.<br><br>Technically, it's nothing ambitious - but it runs as smooth as silk, Where it really shines, however, is the art style Atlus has imbued within the stylised depiction of Tokyo,<br><br>Colours pop off the screen, and character designs are magnificent. Exaggerated expressions and gaudy showbiz glitter make the whole thing look like a playable anime, and the movie-like cutscenes are of amazing quality.<br>
