<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="line-height: 107%; font-size: 13.3333px;" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU">The gameplay for <span style="font-size:12px;line-height: 107%;"><i style="font-size: 12px;">Justice for All</i></span> is relatively similar to the first game. There are investigation sections where you are required to investigate various places and talk to potential witnesses and other related people. Once you have finished investigating all that is required, you move onto the Court Room sections where you Cross Examine witnesses and either press them for more information or present evidence that shows a contradiction to what they’re saying.</span><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"> <br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU">In this game, two mechanics were added that help increase the difficulty and also add a bit of variety. First up, you now have the ability to present the profiles of people, alongside evidence.  This requires you to think a little harder during Cross Examinations because you have more opportunity to get an answer wrong. </span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU">The second mechanic that was added is called, ‘Psychelocks’. Via an item that you receive in-game called a Magatama, you are now able to see ‘locks’ on a person’s heart that show whenever they are hiding something from you. To break these Psychelocks you need the right evidence to present. If you present something wrong you receive a penalty.</span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0cm;margin-bottom:.0001pt"><span style="font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:11.0pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" georgia",serif"="" lang="EN-AU">Aside from the new mechanics, everything remains the same. As always, an Ace Attorney game will test your ability to pay attention to small clues and your critical thinking, rather than your reaction time.<br></span></p>
