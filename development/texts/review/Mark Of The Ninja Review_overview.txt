With almost 40 hours in the last week and the promise of plenty more when I'm finished writing this, it was only a matter of time before I reviewed one of my favourite stealth games of all time, Mark Of The Ninja.<br />
<br />
You don't even need to look at the informative developer's commentary balloons which can be seen floating in the game world to realise just how much work the developers at Klei put into every aspect of the game. For the last three years since it was initially released, critics and reviewers have praised Ninja for its' stealth gameplay, plot twists, and everything in between. Here's why.
