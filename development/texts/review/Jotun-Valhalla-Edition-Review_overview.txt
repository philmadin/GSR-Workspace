<br>Successfully Kickstarted before originally releasing in 2015, <i>Jotun</i> was met with much adoration, thanks to its beautiful hand-drawn aesthetic and loving translation of the ever-adaptable Norse mythology. Now released for home consoles under the moniker of the '<i>Valhalla Edition</i>', <i>Jotun</i> now has the chance of being experienced by a wider audience.<br>
