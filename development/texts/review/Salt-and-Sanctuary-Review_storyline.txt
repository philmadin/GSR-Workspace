Your character starts out on a ship, escorting a Princess across the sea in the hope of peace among their lands. Then that’s about it for a little while. Even as far as downloadable 2D sidescrollers go, there's minimal story given. Instead you come to learn it from what you read and see around you.But it’s hard to care.&nbsp;<p>The locales are so dreary and the world so broadly painted with the same brush that unlike it’s inspiration, it's hard to really sit down and manifest the desire to want to learn more about this world. Occasionally there are some characters and bosses that are both bizarre and unique, but for the most part engaging with them felt more of an obligation.</p>
