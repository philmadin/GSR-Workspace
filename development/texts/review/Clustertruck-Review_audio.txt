<i><br>Clustertruck's </i>sound design shares a lot with its aesthetic. That it to say it doesn't take itself too seriously but suits the game perfectly.<p><br></p><p>Each level has it's own unique sound track that's suitably paced to match the tempo of the game. They're basic, but a lot of fun.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p>Sound effects are minimal but once again suit the concept all too well. From honking trucks to squealing tires to the subtle splat when the player hits an obstacle, it all comes together to create a perfect slice of lunacy.</p>
