<i>Spec Ops: The Line</i> is the latest and most probably the last in the long line of the <i>Spec Ops</i> series, a group of middling, mostly forgotten military shooters that have long since been over shadowed by the likes of <i>Call of Duty</i> and Battlefield. Unlike its predecessors however, <i>Spec Ops: The Line</i> goes beyond the bounds of the standard military shooter and asks some very real and very dark questions.
