<font face="Georgia, serif"><span style="font-size: 13.3333px;">Music is a very important aspect of any Ace Attorney game and what’s featured in <i style="font-size: 13.3333px;">Trials & Tribulations</i> is just as fantastic as you’d expect. The Cross Examination and the Investigation themes provide the perfect accompaniment as you interrogate witnesses and search for clues. Plus, you’ll definitely get hyped up when <i style="font-size: 13.3333px;">Pursuit ~ Caught</i> starts playing as you’re finally cracking the case. Some other standout tracks include <i style="font-size: 13.3333px;">Godot ~ The Fragrance Of Dark Coffee</i> and <i style="font-size: 13.3333px;">Elise Deauxnim ~ Gentle Melody</i>.</span></font>
