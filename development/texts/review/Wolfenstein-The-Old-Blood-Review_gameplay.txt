The gameplay itself is very similar to that of The New Order, which is unsurprising knowing that development began soon after its release. This, however, does not make it any less relevant in terms of providing a solid five hour ‘ish’ single player romp, where the only drawback to combat is deciding on which gun to choose from. <br />
<br />
Players will be placed in large rooms and hallways, where utilising the cover of the objects in the surroundings, picking up ammo, health and armour on the fly and shooting with accuracy provide the only certain path to success. <br />
<br />
Of course, going full Rambo whilst dual-wielding weapons is never to be discouraged. There are also a number of stealth scenarios which effectively break up the action.
