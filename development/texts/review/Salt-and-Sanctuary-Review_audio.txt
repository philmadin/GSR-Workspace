Sounds within the game are fairly standard moans and groans of enemies. Unique enemies have interesting and quirky sounds and Bosses have their own music. As far as 2D side scrollers go, it’s good, not great, but good. The use of silence is common and is effective when there isn’t a lot happening. Again, making use of a weapon in <i>Dark Souls</i> arsenal, without really having the dexterity to wield it effectively.
