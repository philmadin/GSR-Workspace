<i><br>Dragon Quest VII: Fragments of the Forgotten Past</i> is one of the most highly anticipated 3DS games of the year. Originally released for the PlayStation in 2001 (excluding PAL territories), the seventh entry in the long-running and venerated RPG franchise has long been the white whale for fans; a gigantic adventure that has been limited in its availability for 15 years.<br><br><br>Now, thanks to the combined efforts of Nintendo and Square-Enix, <i>Fragments of the Forgotten Past</i> has finally been given a new lease on life. It's an undoubted classic, but has it stood the test of time?<br>
