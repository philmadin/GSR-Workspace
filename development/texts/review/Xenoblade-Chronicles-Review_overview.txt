Over the last decade, JRPGs have been in noticeable decline, as Xenoblade Chronicles advances, will it be the new forefront for the genre?<br />
<br />
JRPG’s were once the gaming industry’s golden goose (thanks mostly to the dominance of Squaresoft), However, that’s not to say they have disappeared completely – and if Xenoblade Chronicles is any indicator, then they’re about to come back in a big way.<br />
<br />
Originally released on Wii – and now available on both the New 3DS and Nintendo eShop – Xenoblade is quite simply one of the best games to emerge from camp Nintendo in years, and proof that there is still plenty of innovation in those old JRPG bones.<br />
