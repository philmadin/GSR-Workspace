<p class="MsoNormal">The story of Fire Emblem: Heroes can perhaps be equated to a combination of Pokemon, and an episode of a Justice League cartoon.&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Your goal is to recruit classic characters from all over Fire Emblem’s long history, and unite them to defeat all evil doers.&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Now this might sound super elaborate and fan-service-y, and that’s because it is. A veteran of the franchise will revel in the opportunity to control their favourite characters again, while new players will have to wade through the lacklustre plot without this added nostalgia.&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></p>
