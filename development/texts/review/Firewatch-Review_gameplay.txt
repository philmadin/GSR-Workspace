<i>Firewatch</i> offers no combat or actions to speak of, many have dubbed it a “walking simulator.” That is not necessarily a bad thing, as the primary gameplay mechanics rely on the game's core strengths of dialogue and exploration.<br><br>You can climb, run, pick up and extract things and also converse on your walkie talkie. It may be simple but they make sense for what the game is; a people watcher. <br>
