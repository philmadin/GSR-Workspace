Set in 1958 Scandinavia, The game follows Otto a boy living in a small village who goes out hunting one day and returns to find his home on fire and his injured mother telling him to flee to safety. while in the wilderness Otto meets a troll, a magical creature believed to be a myth told to children by their parent to keep them in line. Otto and the troll decide to team up to combat the hunters sent to capture the troll and in turn save their home.&lt;br&gt;
