<h3>Know When To Run</h3> Did you hear that surge in music? Then it is time to book it out of there. The enemies are relentless and you will need to break line of sight for them to slow down their search. Manage your stamina, otherwise you will be caught. The sooner you get to a hiding spot, the better off you will be. Most of the time you will be able to avoid enemies by walking around them as they continue their patrols.
