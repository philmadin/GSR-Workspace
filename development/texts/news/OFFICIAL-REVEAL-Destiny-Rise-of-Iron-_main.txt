Bungie has revealed the next greatest adventure for its Destiny players in the form of the new expansion Rise of Iron.<br><br>The expansion sees players unraveling the mystery behind the Iron Lords and an all new threat they must face in the form of an all new enemy faction known as Devil Splicers.<br><br>Features include a large new playable zone (The Plaguelands), a new social space (Felwinter Peak), a new raid, new and updated strikes, all new weapons and gear, and a new PvP mode, maps and features.<br><br>More information will be revealed at E3 next week.<br><br>Destiny: Rise of Iron will launch on PS4 and Xbox One on 20th September 2016.<br>
