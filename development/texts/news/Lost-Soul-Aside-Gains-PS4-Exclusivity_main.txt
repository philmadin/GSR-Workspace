<br><i>Lost Soul Aside</i>, the ambitious independent title that mixes <i>Final Fantasy</i> with <i>Devil May Cry</i>, has caught the eye of Sony, according to its developer, Bing Yang.<br><br><br>Stating that he has now received support from the PlayStation manufacturer as well as other devs (as per his <a href="https://twitter.com/yagnbing">Twitter</a>), it seems that the Unreal Engine 4-powered game will now find a temporarily exclusive home with the PS4, with a potential release for sometime in 2018.<br><br><br>GSR has previously published an interview with Bing Yang, which can be found <a href="http://gamesharkreviews.com/news.php?t=Lost_Soul_Aside_-_Bing_Yang_Interview">here!</a><br>
