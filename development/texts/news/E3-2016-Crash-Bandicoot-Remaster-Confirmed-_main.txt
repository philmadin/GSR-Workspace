Sony have announced today that Crash Bandicoot will be making a return to PlayStation under a special agreement with Activision, who own the original rights. <br><br><i>Crash Bandicoot</i>, <i>Crash Bandicoot: Cortex Strikes Back</i> and <i>Crash Bandicoot: Warped</i> will all be remastered and released on the PlayStation 4 at an unspecified date. <br><br>The announcement was made alongside that Crash will be a new exclusive character on <i>Skylanders: Imaginators</i>. <br>
