Bethesda and ZeniMax Online recently announced the latest adventure for their award winning MMO title The Elder Scrolls Online. The Elder Scrolls Online: Morrowind sees players returning to the iconic island of Vvardenfell where the events of The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind take place.<br><br>Set 700 years before the events of The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind, ESO's latest chapter aims to take you on a nostalgic journey through familiar lands. Players will attempt to solve the mystery of Vivec City's mysterious plague and help to restore its strength.<br><br>The new expansion boasts the largest landmass addition to ESO to date as well as a brand new player class, The Warden. A brand new PvP game mode has also been announced, bringing 4v4v4 combat to the frontier of the Ashlands.<br><br>The Elder Scrolls Online: Morrowind will be available worldwide from June 6th, 2017 for all platforms.<br>
