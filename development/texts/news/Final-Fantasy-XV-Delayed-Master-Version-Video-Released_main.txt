After a turbulent 10 year wait, <i>Final Fantasy</i> <i>XV </i>fans will have to wait just a little bit longer.<br><br>Originally scheduled for a worldwide release on September 30th, the title will now be unleashed for PS4 and Xbox One on November 29th. <br><br>Explaining the cause of the delay, director Hajime Tabata stated that the game is complete, but would have required an extensive day-one patch. His team was unsatisfied with this compromise, and so made the hard choice to hold back in order to work the update into the actual base game.<br><br>To ease the blow, Square-Enix has released a 52 minute video of the current 'Master Version' of the game, showcasing what gamers can expect in the opening hours.<br>
