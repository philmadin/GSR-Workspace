The long awaited sequel to the Mafia series is finally here with Mafia III. <br><br>Mafia III sees players step into the shoes of Lincoln Clay, a man out for revenge against the Italian mafia within the city of New Bordeaux.<br><br>In order to take down this organisation, players will have to build their own mafia comprised of multiple gangs and under bosses throughout the city. Managing these relationships with other mob bosses will be a major focus within the game both in terms of story and playstyle.<br><br>Mafia III is set to release on October 7th, 2016 on PS4, Xbox One and PC.<br>
