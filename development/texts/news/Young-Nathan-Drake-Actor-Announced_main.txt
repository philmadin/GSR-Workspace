Tom Holland (<i>Spiderman: Homecoming</i>) is set to play a young Nathan Drake in the upcoming <i>Uncharted</i> movie. Inspired by the flashback sequence from Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception, the script is set for another rewrite as they adjust to the new direction of the film. <br><br>The film has been stuck in development since 2008 and the casting of Holland will probably push the start of filming to next year.<br>
