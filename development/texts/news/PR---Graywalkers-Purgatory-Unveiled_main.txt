Dreamlords Digital, the minds behind the unique and exciting video game called <i>Graywalkers: Purgatory</i>, is hard at work preparing for Early Access in the second quarter of 2017. <br><br><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;"><i style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">Graywalkers: Purgatory</i>&nbsp;is the first in a series of emergent&nbsp;Strategic and Tactical Turn-based Role-Playing Games&nbsp;set in a stylish supernatural post-apocalyptic world, where parts of Heaven and Hell have merged with Earth in an event called&nbsp;"The Rupture".&nbsp; <br></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">The game was inspired by a combination of the tactical elements of <i style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">XCom</i>, the strategic features of <i style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">Jagged Alliance</i>, and the RPG aspects of classic <i style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">Fallout</i> &amp; <i style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">Wasteland</i>. The game was funded on Kickstarter and is now currently in the alpha stage.&nbsp; It will be released first for PC (Windows, Mac, Linux) and later on will be ported to console (PS4 &amp; Xbox One) <br style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;"></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 14.65pt;">The story happens on the new island of Purgatory, a place made up of displaced land masses from all over the world that have merged together. You play the prophesied leader of the Graywalkers, 36 individuals who are destined to protect mankind across history. Your goal is to unite all the factions together under your banner and lead the war to reclaim the world for humanity against the supernatural forces that have dominated the world.&nbsp;</p>
