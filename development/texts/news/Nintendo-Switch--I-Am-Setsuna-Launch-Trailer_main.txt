<p>Square Enix Ltd., today released a brand new Nintendo Switch gameplay trailer ahead of Friday's launch of the classic-style JRPG adventure I AM SETSUNA. The debut title from all-new Square Enix studio, Tokyo RPG Factory, will be released as part of the Nintendo Switch launch day line-up and will be available for download on 3rd March 2017.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The trailer introduces players to the nostalgic world of I AM SETSUNA and sets the scene for the emotional journey of sacrifice that lies ahead. The trailer showcases the game's classic JRPG gameplay, with simple turn based controls and unique gameplay mechanisms that merge the beloved nostalgic elements from the golden era of JRPGs with the latest technology.</p>
