<i>Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology</i> – It’s been six years since <i>Radiant Historia</i> was released on Nintendo DS in 2011, but it’s time to return to Vainqueur and unlock the time traveling power of the White Chronicle once again in <i>Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology</i>. This beloved classic is an expanded 3DS port of the original game that will contain a great deal of added gameplay/story content, updated presentation, and new ways to experience the adventure! Coming to Nintendo 3DS in early 2018!
