The latest installment in the long running FIFA game series, FIFA 17, was officially announced today at the EA Play Press Conference.<br><br>FIFA 17, now powered by the Frostbite engine (the same engine used for Battlefield), will now contain, for the first time, a dramatic single player story campaign called The Journey. This new story mode follows the trials and tribulations of Alex Hunter as he makes his way to the top of the premier league.<br><br>Other improvements to the game include rewritten set pieces and physical play, more intelligent AI systems and new ways to attack and score.<br><br>Premier league managers will now also make an appearance during matches.<br><br>FIFA 17 launches worldwide on PS3, Xbox 360, PS4, Xbox One and PC on September 29th 2016.<br>
