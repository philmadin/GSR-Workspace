Bungie and Activision Publishing are excited to announce Destiny 2, the blockbuster sequel to the biggest new console video game franchise is set to launch on 8th September 2017.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p></p><p>Bungie and Activision Publishing, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Activision Blizzard, announced Destiny 2, the sequel to the internationally acclaimed Destiny, a first-person action game that takes the player on an epic journey to defend humanity from annihilation.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The official Destiny 2 reveal trailer, "Rally the Troops," debuted a character-driven glimpse at Destiny 2's story today. Additionally, the first hands-on gameplay reveals livestream will be broadcast around the world on May 18. &nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The broadcast will showcase a variety of fun, action-oriented activities within Destiny 2, and will highlight the brand-new, epic adventure waiting for players. With a fresh start for all players right from the opening cinematic, Destiny 2 will expand across the globe with a worldwide release on September 8. &nbsp;Destiny 2 will be available on PlayStation(R)4 systems with additional timed exclusive content*, Xbox One (PlayStation(R)Plus or Xbox Live Gold subscription, respectively, required for significant features), and for the first time in Destiny's history, a new platform to enjoy this universe will be offered. &nbsp;Destiny 2 is coming to PC!</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>"Destiny&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">was the biggest launch of a new console video game franchise ever, and Destiny 2 will welcome a new generation into this action-packed universe," said Activision CEO, Eric Hirshberg. &nbsp;"Along with our incredibly talented partners at Bungie, we are focusing on making Destiny 2 appealing to more fans than ever - with state of the art first person action, an awesome new story, great characters, and innovations that make the game more accessible than ever.
