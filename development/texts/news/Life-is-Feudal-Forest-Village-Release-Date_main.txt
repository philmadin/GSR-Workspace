<p>Developer Mindillusion and publisher Bitbox have announced the upcoming release date for their Middle Ages-era city building game, <i>Life is Feudal: Forest Village</i>. Currently in Early Access, it is set for full release on PC on the 26th of May.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><i style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">Life is Feudal: Forest Village</i> is a feature-rich, town building simulator strategy game with engaging survival mechanics.&nbsp;</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">It&nbsp;</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">challenges players to lead a small group of refugees, forced to begin again on an unknown island. Shape the land and expand with houses, pastures, orchards, farms, windmills and many other real-world experiences. Forage in the forest, hunt for prey, grow crops and domesticate animals for food. As Winter creeps in, you'll need to stock up on firewood, charcoal and warm clothes to survive.</span></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>You are also able to take control of your villagers, zoom down to their level, and work on construction, foraging and management, or just enjoy strolling around town. Forest Village is all about realism, from having to fight disease and malnutrition, to dealing with the specific water and soil requirements of a summer crop, and even getting everyone undercover during a terrifying lightning storm.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>If you've ever dreamed of starting a colony from scratch, this is shaping up to be a game that will allow you to do just that. Happy surviving and re-populating!</p>
