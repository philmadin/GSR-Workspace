<i>The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim</i>, the epic fantasy from Bethesda Game Studios, arrives for Nintendo Switch. The legendary open-world adventure where you can be anyone and do anything, now allows you to play anywhere - at home on your TV or on the go. <i>The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim </i>for the Nintendo Switch console includes all-new exclusive features like motion control for combat and lockpicking, outfits and gear from <i>The Legend of Zelda</i> franchise, and amiibo compatibility. Coming to Nintendo Switch Holiday 2017.
