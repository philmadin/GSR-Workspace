From the developers of<i> Heavy Rain</i> and <i>Beyond: Two Soul</i>s, comes the upcoming game <i>Detroit: Become Human.<br></i><br>The story follows several characters and is played from a third person perspective. The main characters so far are Connor and Kara, who are both androids with very different paths. <br><br>Based on choices made in the game, not all of the characters may make it to the end. The story evolves with each decision, resulting in a unique playthrough each time.<br><br><i>Detroit: Become Human</i> is a PlayStation 4 exclusive from Quantic Dream and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. No release date has been announced yet.<br>
