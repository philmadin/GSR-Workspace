<i>Etrian Odyssey V: Beyond the Myth</i> – The fifth main installment of the long-running <i>Etrian Odyssey</i> series is finally arriving! <i>Etrian Odyssey V: Beyond the Myth</i> chronicles the quest of a new adventurer as he or she attempts to reach the top of the towering Yggdrasil Tree. For it is said that whoever braves the dangers of the dreaded Yggdrasil Labyrinth and reaches the summit will have their greatest wish fulfilled… Coming to Nintendo 3DS in fall 2017!
