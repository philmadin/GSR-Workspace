<p>SQUARE ENIX today announced that the upcoming highly anticipated Action-RPG, NieR: Automata, will be available on Steam globally beginning March 17, 2017.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Developed in collaboration with PlatinumGames Inc., NieR: Automata tells the story of androids 2B, 9S, and A2, and their ferocious battle to reclaim a machine-driven dystopia abandoned by humankind and overrun by machine lifeforms.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Those who pre-purchase the Steam Day One Edition will receive an exclusive "Valve Character Accessory," as well as other Day One Edition bonus content, including the Machine Mask Accessory, Grimoire Weiss Pod, Retro Grey Pod Skin, Retry Red Pod Skin, and Cardboard Pod Skin.</p>
