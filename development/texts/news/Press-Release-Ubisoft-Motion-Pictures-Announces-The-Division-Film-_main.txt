Ubisoft Motion Pictures, the film and television studio of Ubisoft®, has announced a new project, “<i>The Division</i>” with Academy Award® nominees Jessica Chastain (The Martian, Interstellar, The Help, Zero Dark Thirty) and Jake Gyllenhaal (End of Watch, Nightcrawler, Southpaw) attached.<p><br></p><p><br><br>“We are excited to collaborate with Jessica and Jake, two of Hollywood’s most talented actors&nbsp; and perfect creative collaborators to help bring <i>Tom Clancy’s The Division</i>™ to the big screen,” said Gerard Guillemot, Chief Executive Officer, Ubisoft Motion Pictures. “Attaching Jake and Jessica is part of our development philosophy of working closely with top talent from the earliest stages to collaborate on a high quality film,” added Matt Phelps, Vice President, Ubisoft Motion Pictures.</p><p><br></p><p><br><br>Ubisoft Motion Pictures will develop with Gyllenhaal’s and Riva Marker’s (Beasts of No Nation) Nine Stories and Chastain’s Freckle Films. The announcement comes after the critically-acclaimed <i>Tom Clancy’s The Division</i>™ video game, developed by Ubisoft’s Massive Entertainment studio, sold more copies in its first 24 hours of availability than any previous title in Ubisoft’s history* and recorded the biggest first week ever for a new video game franchise**. &nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br><br>Ubisoft Motion Pictures and New Regency’s <i>Assassin’s Creed</i>®, starring Academy Award® nominee Michael Fassbender, has recently completed principal photography. <i>Assassin’s Creed </i>will be released by 20th Century Fox on December 21, 2016. Other Ubisoft Motion Pictures projects currently in development include: <i>Splinter Cell </i>at New Regency with Tom Hardy attached, <i>Ghost Recon</i> with Warner Brothers, <i>Watch Dogs</i> with Sony Pictures Entertainment and New Regency, as well as <i>Rabbids</i> with Sony Pictures Entertainment. <br><br><br><br>* Based upon internal estimates for Ubisoft’s <i>Tom Clancy’s The Division</i> worldwide sales data on all<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; platforms as of March 9, 2016.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br>* Estimated world-wide revenue from physical and digital full game sales (including gold edition)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; only, according to GFK Chart-Track, first parties, retail customer sell-through and internal<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; estimates through end March 12th 2016 at current exchange rates.</p>
