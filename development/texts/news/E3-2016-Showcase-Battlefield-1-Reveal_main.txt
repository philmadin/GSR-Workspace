The wait is over! Battlefield 1 was revealed in all its glory this morning at the EA Play press conference. <br><br>The game will contain another single player campaign and 64 player battlefield combat.<br><br>Other features include dynamic weather that changes unpredictably over the course of the battle, enhanced levels of destruction, and an impressive roster of vehicles and weapons at the player's disposal including airships, boats, tanks, motorbikes (with side cars) and horses.<br><br>Battlefield 1 will release October 21st, 2016 on PS4, Xbox One and PC.<br>
