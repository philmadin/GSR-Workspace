As the Christmas event wraps up, Blizzard has continued to roll out new content with its latest map, Oasis.<br><br>It is a control map that introduces the new element of jump pads to Overwatch. This doesn't just improve mobility but can be used in conjunction with abilities and ultimate's to change their impact. Players also need to be aware of traffic that can instantly kill your hero while learning the map's layout.<br><br>While previously available on the public test realm for PC, it is now available on all platforms for players to enjoy and is the featured map for Arcade.<br><br>
