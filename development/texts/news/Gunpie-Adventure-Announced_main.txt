<p>An ancient power is unleashed bringing chaos into the world as NEXON Korea Corporation reveals an immersive first-person shooter (FPS) with role-playing game (RPG) elements in <i>Gunpie Adventure</i>. The game is available in limited release for iOS and Android devices in Australia, Canada, Finland, Indonesia, Macau (only iOS), and Singapore and will launch globally later this year.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Gunpie Adventure </i>combines a modern FPS experience with RPG elements in a fast-paced, action adventure game. When the Sirius Foundation awakens a power that releases ancient monsters, players must explore and uncover prehistoric artifacts to engage in battle and restore peace to the world. Vast excavation sites feature artifacts with energy that players can absorb to improve combat power, increase weapons arsenal, and provide access to a wide selection of realistic vehicles.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Official Gunpie Adventure forum: <a class="releaselink" href="https://m.nexon.com/forum/159" target="_new"><gp_link type="https" loc="//m.nexon.com/forum/159">https://m.nexon.com/forum/159</gp_link></a></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Official Facebook page: &nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/GunpieAdventure/ ">https://www.facebook.com/GunpieAdventure/ </a></p>
