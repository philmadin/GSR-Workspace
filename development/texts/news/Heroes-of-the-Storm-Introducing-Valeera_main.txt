Blizzard have posted a sneak peak of Valeera, a new hero joining the roster for Heroes of the Storm.<br><br>Valeera is a character from the Warcraft universe and represents the Rogue class in Hearthstone. Her skillset includes: Vanish, Sinister Strike, Blade Flurry, Eviscerate, Smoke Bomb and Cloak of Shadows. As this is only on the PTR, expect balance changes as players experiment with her playstyle. <br><br>Alongside the release of Valeera, she is also receiving extra skins to coincide with the Lunar New Year event taking place across most Blizzard games. There are also new achievements and mounts added as well. The patch will more than likely go live next week, as Blizzard hasn't made any official announcement yet.<br>
