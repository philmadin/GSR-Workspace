The farther you travel, the better the wormholes get. They are of 4 rarities:
No Rings
Single Ring
Double Ring
Double Ring and pattern
The more light years you travel, the higher the chance these higher-rarity wormholes will appear. The rarest wormhole only starts to appear after 2500 light years.
Each Wormhole has a designated colour:
White Wormholes 
Blue Wormholes 
Red Wormholes 
Yellow Wormholes 
Green Wormholes
