<h3>Counter to the Conqueror:</h3> The Assassins are a really good counter to conqueror. They can easily side dodge, hit the Conqueror and potentially spin circles around him while he stands helpless. Look out for his charged heavy and dodge often. Be wary of your surroundings. Do NOT be cornered by a conqueror. He will make you pay.His heavy attacks can easily be parried leading to confirmed damage.
