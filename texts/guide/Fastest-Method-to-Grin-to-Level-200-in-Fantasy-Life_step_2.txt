What you will be doing is crafting a Rainbow Gelato, over and over again. So you’ll need to buy the ingredients in bulk. You will need about 45-50 stacks of 99 each if you want to go from level 100 to 200. The ingredients are: 


- Giga Water (acquire from Furlin’s Grotto on Origin Island)
- Rainbow Apple (acquire from Elderwood Village)
- Extra Creamy Milk (acquire from butcher in Castele Shopping District)
