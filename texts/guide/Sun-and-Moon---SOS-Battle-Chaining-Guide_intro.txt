Over the generations, chaining Pokémon has changed quite a bit. From chaining using the radar in <i>Diamond</i> and <i>Pearl</i>, to the Dex Nav in <i>Omega Ruby</i> and <i>Alpha Sapphire</i>. <i>Sun</i> <i>and</i> <i>Moon</i> introduce yet another version of chaining Pokémon in SOS Battles. This guide will teach you the very basics so you can get your own shiny, hidden ability, or IV perfect pal!
