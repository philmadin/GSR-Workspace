<h3>Typhon Abilities</h3> When you unlock the psychoscope in Psychotronics, you will be able to scan enemies which will unlock new abilities. 
The first abilities that you should unlock is Mimick Matter, Machine Mind and Phantom Shift. 
Phantom Shift will allow you to survive, Mimick Matter will allow you to cram through tiny spaces and Machine Mind will allow you control enemy turrets and robots.
