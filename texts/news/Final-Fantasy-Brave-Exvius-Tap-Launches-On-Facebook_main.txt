Square Enix has launched its first Instant Games experience, <i>Final Fantasy Brave Exvius Tap!</i>. Based on the characters and world of <i>Final Fantasy Brave Exviu</i>s, this game is designed for Facebook with simplified battles and controls.<br><br>The goal of the game is to quickly tap and defeat a series of monsters within a certain time limit. There are over 1000 stages and twenty-five characters to unlock. Players can also join their friends in a raid system to tackle more difficult bosses. Also included, is an online ranking system to compare your stats with your friends.<br><br><i>Final Fantasy Brave Exvius Tap!</i> is available now via Messenger and News Feed on mobile and desktop.
