<p>MecurySteam has announced their first major update to their game, <i>Raiders of the Broken Planet</i>. They said they plan to 'evolve and develop' the game in tangent with its audience thanks to player feedback. This update will change the way some of the players experience the game.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Previously, <i>Raiders of the Broken Planet</i> had one free Prologue and four adventure driven campaigns. The free Prologue players were formerly unable to access the economy of the game, unlike those who purchased the Founders Pack or Alien Myths campaign. This will be changed in the latest update when the in-game economy will be open to all.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Prologu<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">e players will now be able to use the Gold, Faction Points, and Character Points not only to unlock characters and build weapons but also uncover pieces of lore.&nbsp;</span></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Characters Mikan, Dr Kuzmann, and Ginebra, which before were reserved for Alien Myths players, will now be available for anyone to unlock. However, players who do purchase Alien Myths can experience the 'first epic story of season one' and obtain Shae, the lethal sniper, who is the campaign's exclusive character.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>MercurySteam will be making more changes in the coming weeks and months to ensure that the players have the flexibility and options to properly enjoy the game's content.</p>
