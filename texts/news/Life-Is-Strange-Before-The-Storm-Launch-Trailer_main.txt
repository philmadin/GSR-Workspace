<i>The Life Is Strange </i>prequel titled '<i>Before The Storm</i>', featuring fan favourite Chloe Price, launched on the 31st of August. The official PlayStation YouTube channel released the launch trailer for the game.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The emotionally hard hitting trailer features the indie rock style music the series is known for, while also introducing players to the pivotal character of Rachel Amber. Their relationship and its subsequent impact on Chloe's life is sure to be the primary focus of the three episode mini-series.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The trailer highlights Chloe's identity crisis and mental instability due to traumatic events recently occurring in her life. These include her father's death, the abandonment by her best friend Max Caulfield and her ongoing struggle to find herself.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The first episode is currently available on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC.</p>
