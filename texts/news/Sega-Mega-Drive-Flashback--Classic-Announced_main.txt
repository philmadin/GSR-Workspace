AtGames(R) Digital Media Ltd., a leader in interactive entertainment products, today announced their latest classic gaming releases: <b>Sega Mega Drive Flashback</b> and <b>Classic Game Console</b>. Each console includes an amazing selection of built-in Sega Mega Drive games, respectively, as well as an impressive range of innovative features. The systems, which are headlined by a brand new premium high definition (HD) output model, will be in Australian stores for spring 2017.<br><br><p class="x_MsoNormal">"These upcoming products are our best hardware line-up ever," says Dr. Ping-Kang Hsiung, CEO of AtGames. "Building on our long history of delivering value-packed, nostalgic products for our customers, this new line-up takes classic gaming to a stunning new level."</p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="x_MsoNormal">The all-new <b>Sega Mega Drive Flashback</b> has a stunning new design, reminiscent of the original Sega Mega Drive console, and features 85 built-in games as well as an integrated cartridge port that plays almost all of your favourite Sega Mega Drive and Genesis original cartridges. Built-in all-time favourites include the Sonic(TM) series, Mortal Kombat (TM)series, Phantasy Star(TM) series, and Shining Force(TM) series games. Two new high performance 2.4ghz wireless controllers, styled after the Sega Mega Drive originals, are included, as well as two legacy controller ports for optional wired gamepads. Other new features for the Sega Mega Drive Flashback include 720p HDMI output, scanline filtering, and a save/pause/rewind feature for every game.</p><p class="x_MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:12.0pt">The <b style="margin-bottom:12.0pt">Classic Game Console</b> returns in a new value-priced edition, complete with 81 all-time favourites. Amazing new additions to the game line-up include role-playing and strategy legends, Shining Force(TM), and Shining Force II(TM). Two wired controllers, styled after the Sega Mega Drive originals, are included, as well as two legacy controller ports to add your own optional original controllers. <br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:12.0pt"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:12.0pt">With an expanded product line, new titles, and smart new features, AtGames is creating a modern home for all things retro gaming. Celebrating classic gaming heritage has never been easier, whether for nostalgic old-timers or newer generations looking for a taste of the best of the past. Expect these products and many more from AtGames in stores this holiday season and beyond.</p>
