<p>Twenty years ago, Japanese toymaker Bandai unleashed to the world one of the most popular toy fads that still has a place in every 90s kid's heart, the <i>Tamagotchi</i>. Throughout the years, as the <i>Tamagotchi </i>waned in popularity, Bandai often enhanced features in the toy and even tried producing Tamagotchi games for the Nintendo handheld consoles. 2018 will see Bandai Namco launch <i>Tamagotchi's </i>best idea yet, <i>My Tamagotchi Forever</i>, a free-to-play smartphone/tablet game with an AR element.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The trailer below showcases interaction with the <i>Tamagotchi </i>virtual pet in the home environment by utilising the smartphone's camera. Although not actual gameplay video, we can expect the virtual pet, like its 1990s counterpart, to leave virtual droppings in the AR environment for you to pick up.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Both Android and iOS devices are getting a version of <i>My Tamagotchi Forever.&nbsp;</i><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">The </span><a href="https://www.mytamagotchiforever.com/">official website</a><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"> is also holding a competition at the moment to create a new character for the game.</span></p>
