With the <a href="http://gamesharkreviews.com/news.php?t=2017_Tokyo_Game_Show_Details">Tokyo Game Show</a> wrapped up for 2017, fans of Vanillaware's <i>Dragon's Crown</i> were treated to an announcement from Sony that <i>Dragon's Crown Pro</i> will be released for the PlayStation 4 on January 25th, 2018. The 'Pro' version of the side-scrolling RPG will now support 4K and cross-play with the PlayStation 3 and the PS Vita. The date is a confirmed Japanese release date, yet there is no news on when the game will be available in other countries.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Watch the official Japanese announcement trailer below.</p>
