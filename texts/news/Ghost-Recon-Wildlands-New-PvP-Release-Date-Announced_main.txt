<p>Just a couple of days ago, Ubisoft announced that the new PvP mode update for <i>Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands</i>, Ghost War, will be available October 10th.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>This new mode features two groups of players taking part in a 4v4 team deathmatch experience. Teams choose from a variety of classes across three categories and use strategy and experience from the main game to help fulfil their specific roles. There's a ton of customisation options within the twelve distinct classes which include weapons and varied characteristics. There are also eight unique maps and new PvP mechanics such as suppressing fire and sound markers to make the battle more intense.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Ghost War is free to anyone who owns the base game and the development team intends to bring regular post-launch updates so they can improve the PvP experience. Players can look forward to more maps, classes, and modes to be added in the future.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">Check out the trailer below.</span></p>
