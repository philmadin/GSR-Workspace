<p>Gearbox Publishing has teamed up with tinyBuild and Dynamic Pixels this week and are bringing <i>Hello Neighbor</i> to retail. The game is now available to pre-order on Xbox One at some US retailers. It will be coming to international retailers as well, but they have yet to be announced.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Hello Neighbor</i> is a game where you break into your neighbour's house to discover what secret&nbsp;he's been hiding in his basement. You must be careful, though, because the Neighbour is watching. The Neighbor analyses your habits continually and counters them in increasingly unique ways.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Hello Neighbor</i> will be available on December 8, 2017.
