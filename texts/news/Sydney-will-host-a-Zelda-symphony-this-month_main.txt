<i>The Legend of Zelda: Symphony of the Goddesses</i> will make its way down to the Sydney Opera House this month on October 29. Sydney will be the last city to host the Australian leg of the symphony's 2017 <a href="https://zelda-symphony.com/pages/schedule">tour</a> (with Perth and Melbourne holding concerts months earlier). The Sydney Opera House will accommodate fans by holding two concerts on the same day: a 3:30 pm show and an 8:00 pm show (which happens to be listed as "Almost Gone!").<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Fans of <i>The Legend of Zelda</i> can relive all their favourite moments from the series through the symphony's new multimedia concert experience. The two-hour event will showcase music from select <i>Zelda</i> titles including <i>A Link Between Worlds, Twilight Princess HD, Majora's Mask 3D</i> and classics, <i>Ocarina of Time 3D</i> and <i>A Link to the Past.</i></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Standard tickets start from $99.00 AUD and are available now from the Sydney Opera House <a href="https://www.sydneyoperahouse.com/events/whats-on/classical-music/2017/the-legend-of-zelda-tickets.html">website</a>.</p>
