<p>Ubisoft has announced today that <i>Rayman Legends: Definitive Edition</i> is coming to Nintendo Switch on the 12th of September, 2017. Originally released back in 2013 on a variety of platforms, <i>Rayman Legends</i>&nbsp;won multiple awards for artistic and musical achievements and was also named 'Platformer of the Year'.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Rayman Legends</i>&nbsp;follows Rayman and his friends as they discover a mysterious tent filled with a series of captivating paintings. Upon looking closer, they notice each painting seems to tell the story of a mythical world - a world that they are suddenly sucked into. The gang must run, jump and fight their way through each world to save the day, and discover the secrets of each legendary painting.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The Definitive Edition includes the critically acclaimed <i>Rayman Legends</i>&nbsp;alongside a bunch of yet-to-be-named exclusive content. It also takes full advantage of the Switch's capabilities, with 4-player wireless multiplayer and touch gameplay. Taking out enemies, manipulating platforms, cutting ropes and more has never been easier, and can now be done on the run.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Rayman has been around for over 30 years and just keeps getting better with age. The series has sold well over 25 million copies since its inception, and <i>Rayman Legends</i>&nbsp;continues the fine form with an average score of over 90%. If you've never played a Rayman game before, there is no time like the present!</p>
