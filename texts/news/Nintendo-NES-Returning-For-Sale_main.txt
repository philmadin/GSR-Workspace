<i>Nintendo</i> has surprised everyone with the announcement of the <i>NES</i> making a return to stores. The <i>Nintendo Classic Mini: NES</i> will launch November 10 and cost $99.95.<br><br>The console fits in the palm of your hand and come's with 30 pre-loaded games. Not included in the packaging is an AC adapter, which is needed for the USB cable. The <i>Nintendo Classic Mini: NES Controller</i>, bought separately, can allow a second player to join in on the fun. The controller can also be used on the <i>Wii U</i> or <i>Wii</i> to play <i>Virtual NES</i> games.<br><br>Players worried about the lack of saves can rejoice, as each game now has multiple suspend points, allowing anyone to pick up the game from where they left off.
