Blizzard has flicked the switch and for those that have pre-purchased <i>World of Warcraft: Legion</i> digitally, you can now create a Demon Hunter, the newest class available for the MMORPG. <br><br>Demon Hunters can play through their new starting area in Mardum and the Vault of the Wardens. Once completed, you will then be sent to your faction's capital city where you can join the fight for Azeroth. <br><br>Characters above level 98 will receive a quest that takes you to the Broken Shore. Characters above level 10 can still participate and help defend Azeroth from the invading demon army.<br><br><i>World of Warcraft: Legion</i> launches August 30. <br>
