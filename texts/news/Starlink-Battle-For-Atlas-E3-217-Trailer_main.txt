Watch the world premiere of <i>Starlink: Battle for Atlas</i> and travel deep into space to protect the Atlas star system from enemies!<br><br>In <i>Starlink: Battle for Atlas</i>, you're part of a group of heroic interstellar pilots, dedicated to free the Atlas star system from the Forgotten Legends, an evil robot force. <i>Starlink: Battle for Atlas</i> offers a whole new gameplay experience: build your own modular Starship model, mount it on your controller, and take part in epic space combats. Equip your model with different parts and your Starship changes in game in real time. Assemble your fleet, mix and match your pilots, Starships and weapons to create your own play style and defeat the enemy.
