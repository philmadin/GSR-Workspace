The latest game in the Mass Effect series, Mass Effect Andromeda, enjoyed some screen time during the EA Play Press Conference today.<br><br>The game has been described as an entirely new story in the Mass Effect universe, focusing on the exploration of the Andromeda Galaxy to try and find a new home for human kind.<br><br>Mass Effect Andromeda is scheduled for an early 2017 release on PS4, PC and Xbox One.<br>
