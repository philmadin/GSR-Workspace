<p>A new immersive VR experience is coming this month for arachnophobes and arachnophiles alike. <i>Attack of the Bugs</i> lets you battle hordes of giant creepy crawlies in traditional VR shooter style, as well as gather resources and strategically command your soldiers to help defeat the evil bug Queen.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The game will be playable in a mission campaign mode and in an infinite mode with competitive leaderboards for the best bug exterminators. There is a chance to craft super-weapons and make use of multiple weapon types to strategically defeat each different bug type. With insects flanking you from all sides, quick thinking and utilising the objects around you will ensure your survival.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>"As VR gamers ourselves, we wanted to create something unique that not only used the power of the emerging technology to transport you to a different world, but also brought something new and compelling to the table as a game," says Francois Bertrand, co-founder of Empty Clip Studios and Lead Designer on the project.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Battle your fear with <i>Attack of the Bugs</i>, available on HTC Vive and Oculus Rift via Steam on November 29th, 2017.</p>
