<p class="MsoNormal">Sony Interactive Entertainment Australia and Polyphony Digital Inc. are pleased to announce that Mount Panorama Motor Racing Circuit in Bathurst, Australia, will feature in the upcoming PlayStation 4 exclusive, <i>Gran Turismo Sport</i>.</p><p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Bathurst is the home of Australian motorsport and has a rich history dating back to 1938, when the Australian Grand Prix was first held on the track. Mount Panorama is now home to the FIA accredited Bathurst 12 Hour and Bathurst 1000 races among others.</p><p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p>The team at Polyphony has painstakingly recreated the 4 mile circuit, bringing to life every twist, turn and incline, to challenge even the biggest racing fan. Next generation 3D modelling technology and rendering, coupled with power of the PlayStation 4 will recreate the iconic track in stunning detail. Track details become crisper and clearer in 4K when racers play on PlayStation 4 Pro.<br><br><i>Gran Turismo Sport</i> launches in Spring 2017, exclusive to PlayStation 4. <span style="font-family: Calibri" lang="EN-US"><br></span>
