<p>This week, publisher Rising Star Games and game developer Undercoders announced that <i>Conga Master</i> will be coming to the Nintendo Switch. Previously released on PC, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One, this version will be titled <i>Conga Master Party</i>.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Conga Master Party</i> allows up to four players to travel through different nightclubs with their chosen party-goer in a quest to create the most epic conga line ever. The longer the line, the easier it is to add more people - but beware of stinky pigs who stink up your line.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>There is a ton of new content added to the Switch version, including two new exclusive multiplayer modes, '1, 2, Conga' and 'Just Conga'. Some other new features include amiibo support, which unlock themed costumes; two new stages, 'Conga Burger' and 'Conga's Palace'; and 10 new playable characters, including Richie the high-roller, Hotdog the dancing hotdog, and Dr. H8.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Conga&nbsp;Master Party</i>&nbsp;game will be released digitally throughout the United States and in European territories on September 28.</p><p><br></p>
