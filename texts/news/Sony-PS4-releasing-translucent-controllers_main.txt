Sony is getting ready to release a new line of PlayStation Dualshock 4 Wireless Controllers. The limited-edition designs, appropriately named 'Crystal', 'Red Crystal' and 'Blue Crystal' feature a frosted translucent top panel.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Although the bottom sides are solid in colour, who wouldn't like a good look at their controller's inner nuts and bolts? No changes were made to the controller's revolutionary technology but the aesthetic changes have reminded many of the nostalgic controller trends of the late 1990s and early 2000s.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Pre-orders are already available in North America and the Australian PlayStation <a href="https://www.playstation.com/en-au/explore/accessories/dualshock-4-wireless-controller/">webpage</a> has made it <i>crystal clear</i> they'll be "Available October 2017".</p></p>
