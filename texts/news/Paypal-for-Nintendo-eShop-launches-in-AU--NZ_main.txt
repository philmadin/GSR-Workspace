<p>Nintendo has just announced that customers in Australia and New Zealand can now use Paypal as a method of payment on their Nintendo eShop. This was introduced in the United States and Canada back in August.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Now, customers purchasing digital games and content for their Nintendo Switch will be directed to a checkout screen with the new Paypal option. Initially, you must set up your payment method by linking your Paypal account with your Nintendo Account via your PC or smart device. Conveniently, this sets up your account for future purchases. Customers must ensure the country setting on their Nintendo account matches the country setting of their Paypal account.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>This is limited to users who are 18 years or older.</p>
