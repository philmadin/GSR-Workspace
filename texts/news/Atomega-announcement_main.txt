<p>Ubisoft has just announced that their newest online multiplayer FPS will be available on <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/app/619880/ATOMEGA/">Steam</a> next Tuesday (September 19th). <i>Atomega</i>, developed by Reflections, takes place at the end of time where super advanced post-biological life-forms known as 'Exoforms' compete for fun and dominance. As the player, you must acquire mass which is used to evolve your Exoform from the nimble Atom to the godlike Omega. You can collect mass by competing in 'firefights':&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Eight players battle for supremacy lasting 10 minutes each round. The game was developed to be easy to play yet hard to master so you may find yourself engrossed for hours.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Watch the official Atomega announcement trailer below.</p>
