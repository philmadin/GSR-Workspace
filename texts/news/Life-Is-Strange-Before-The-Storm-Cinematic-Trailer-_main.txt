Square Enix have unveiled <i>Life Is Strange: Before The Storm</i>, the next game in the bestselling franchise. The game was unveiled with a new cinematic trailer as part of the Xbox E3 Briefing and the first of three episodes will be released on Xbox One.<br><br><i>Life Is Strange: Before The Storm </i>is set in Arcadia Bay, three years before the events of the first game in the series. Players will take on the role of rebellious 16 year-old Chloe Price, who forms an unlikely friendship with Rachel Amber, a beautiful and popular girl destined for success. When Rachel's world is turned upside down by a family secret, it takes this new-found alliance to give each other the strength to overcome their demons.<br><br>The first of three episodes will be released on August 31st, 2017 on Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and PC.<br><br>
