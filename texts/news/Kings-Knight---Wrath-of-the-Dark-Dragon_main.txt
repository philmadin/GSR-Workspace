A new action RPG mobile game from the <i>Final Fantasy XV </i>universe for iOS and Android devices. Within <i>Final Fantasy XV </i>, Noctis and his friends can often be found playing this title during their downtime. <br><br>The title is a nod to one of Square Enix's earliest games, <i>King's Knight</i>, which predates the <i>Final Fantasy</i> series. Originally launched 30 years ago, <i>King's Knight</i> was a scrolling shooting game for the Nintendo Entertainment System that was developed by Hironobu Sakaguchi and Nobuo Uematsu.
