<br><i>Fallen Legion</i> brings an intense RPG experience, where every battle is a heart-pounding fight and each gut-wrenching choice changes the world, exclusively to PS4 and Vita. <br><br><br><i>Fallen Legion’s</i> furious battle system has players control up to four characters simultaneously in real-time combat. Managing character stamina and mixing attacks by switching characters is the key to creating devastating combos.<br><br><br>The game’s intensity remains high between fights where you have to make split-second decisions to rule your kingdom. Your choices grant your characters buffs immediately in battle and have a permanent impact in the world.<br><br>
