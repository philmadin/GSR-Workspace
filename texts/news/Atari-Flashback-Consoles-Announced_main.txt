AtGames(R) Digital Media Ltd., a leader in interactive entertainment products, alongside Atari(R), one of the world's most recognized publishers and producers of interactive entertainment, today announced their latest classic gaming releases: <b>Atari Flashback(R) 8 Gold</b> and A<b>tari Flashback(R) 8 Classic Game Console</b>. Each console includes an amazing selection of built-in Atari 2600 games, as well as an impressive range of innovative features. The systems, which are headlined by a brand new premium high definition (HD) output model, will be in Australian stores for spring 2017.<br><br><p class="x_MsoNormal">"We are excited to partner with AtGames again to release three new gaming systems," said Fred Chesnais, Chief Executive Officer, Atari.&nbsp; "The new hardware includes a robust collection of classic games and features which will give our audiences many options to relive the golden age of gaming at home or on the go." </p><p class="x_MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal">The brand-new <b>Atari Flashback(R) 8 Gold</b> features 120 built-in all-time favourites. These games include iconic Atari 2600 classics like Missile Command(R), Asteroids(R), Centipede(TM), and Millipede(TM), as well as many other legends like Konami's Frogger, Activision's Pitfall! and River Raid(TM) and Taito's Space Invaders. Two all new, high performance 2.4ghz wireless controllers, styled after the Atari 2600 originals, are included, as well as two legacy controller ports for optional wired joysticks or paddles. Other new features for the Atari Flashback 8 Gold include 720p HDMI output, scan line filtering, and a save/pause/rewind feature for every game.</p><p class="x_MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal">The <b>Atari Flashback(R) 8 Classic Game Console</b> returns in a new value-priced edition, complete with 105 all-time favourites. Two wired controllers, styled after the Atari 2600 originals, are included, as well as two legacy controller ports to add your own optional wired joysticks or paddles. </p><p class="x_MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="x_MsoNormal">"These upcoming products are our best hardware line-up yet," says Dr. Ping-Kang Hsiung, CEO of AtGames.
