<p>As part of their 2017 E3 press conference, Ubisoft have given us an extended look at the gameplay of <i>South Park: The Fractured But Whole</i>. Taking place in the iconic Peppermint Hippo Strip Club, The New Kid and Captain Diabetes explore the club to uncover more information about missing cats and an unknown evil force.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The footage gives us insight into how the mechanics work in the turn-based team combat system, showing us a battle between the superheros and two drunken VIPs. It also showcases unique abilities and ultimate powers in action.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>The Fractured But Whole</i>&nbsp;is the highly anticipated sequel to the successful <i>Stick of Truth</i>, and shifts the satirical focus from medieval fantasy to superhero movies. It releases on the 17th of October for Xbox One, PS4 and PC.</p>
