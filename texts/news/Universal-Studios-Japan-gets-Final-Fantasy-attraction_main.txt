Universal Studios Japan has <a href="http://www.usj.co.jp/universal-cool-japan2018/">announced</a> their upcoming 2018 attractions and it's awesome. As part of its 'Universal Cool Japan' project, we're going to see Japanese game franchises <i>Final Fantasy</i> and <i>Monster Hunter</i> get attractions.&nbsp;<i>Monster Hunter</i> had previously been a feature at the amusement park but this will be <i>Final Fantasy</i>'s first time. Not much information for the <i>Final Fantasy </i>attraction was given, however, the park's promotional poster uses an image of <i>FF VII </i>protagonist Cloud.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Other notable attractions to headline the park next year are anime/manga cultural icons <i>Great Detective Conan</i> and <i>Sailor Moon</i>. <i>Great Detective Conan</i>, <i>Final Fantasy </i>and <i>Monster Hunter </i>are set to open their attractions on January 19th. <i>Sailor Moon</i> will have a later open date during spring.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Don't put it off too long to visit USJ as you only have until June 24th to experience the magic.
