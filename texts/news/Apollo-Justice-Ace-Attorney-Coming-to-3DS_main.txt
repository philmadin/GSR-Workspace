<p><i>Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney</i>, the fourth game in the <i>Ace Attorney</i>&nbsp;series, has just been announced for release on the 3DS. This game was originally launched for the DS back in 2008 and has since been recently re-released in HD for iOS and Android. Now, with updated graphics and the option to play in Japanese, this game will enthrall new and old players alike.</p><p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">&nbsp;It will be available to download from the Nintendo eShop&nbsp;this November.</span><br></p>
