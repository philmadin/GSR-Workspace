Coming in 2018 is the latest map for <i>Overwatch</i>, BlizzardWorld. The map is an amusement park based on games released by <i>Blizzard</i>. It will feature <i>Heroes of the Storm</i>, <i>World of Warcraft,</i> <i>Starcraft</i> and even <i>The Lost Vikings</i>. <br><br>Alongside the release of the map, eight new skins for various characters will be included in normal loot boxes. They can be earned normally or purchased outright. <br><br>BlizzardWorld will soon be available on the Public Test Realm on PC.<br>
