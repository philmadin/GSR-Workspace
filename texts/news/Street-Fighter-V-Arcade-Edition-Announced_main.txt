<p>A couple days ago, Capcom announced that <i>Street Fighter V: Arcade Edition</i> will be released exclusively for PlayStation 4 and PC January 16, 2018. This updated version will include two new modes, the first two seasons of DLC characters, and other gameplay-related content.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Street Fighter V: Arcade Edition</i> will contain Arcade Mode, Extra Battle Mode, new V-trigger moves and a gallery alongside the main game. It will also have a completely redesigned User Interface.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>In Arcade Mode, players can choose from six different paths and battle CPU opponents to discover unique endings based on their performance. Extra Battle Mode offers players to be able to use their Fight Money in a new series of time exclusive challenges for the chance to obtain exclusive outfits. If players complete all four challenges each month, they receive a special premium costume exclusive to the mode.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Current <i>Street Fighter V</i> players will be receiving all of this new content for free except for the DLC characters. These characters can be accessed using earned Fight Money or purchased in-game currency.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Check out the reveal trailer below!</p>
