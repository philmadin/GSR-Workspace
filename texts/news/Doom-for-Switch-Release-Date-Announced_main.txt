<p>Back in September, we got exciting news out of nowhere that <i>DOOM</i>&nbsp;would be coming to Nintendo Switch. Today we can announce that said game will be releasing on November 10.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The announcement came as part of an id Software developer video that was released today by Nintendo, featuring creative director Hugo Martin and executive producer Marty Stratton. The guys talk about what we can expect in the Switch port of <i>DOOM</i>, including the full single-player campaign, arcade mode and an entire suite of multiplayer maps and modes. As a nice addition, the Switch version will also ship with all previously released DLC content.</p><p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><br></span></p><p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><br></span></p><p>They also go into further depth about working with developer Panic Button to ensure gameplay feels as fast, brutal and true to the experience that we have come to expect from the&nbsp;<i>DOOM&nbsp;</i>franchise, especially on a console that isn't particularly known for hectic FPS action.
