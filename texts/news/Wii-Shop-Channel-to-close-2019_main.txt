Nintendo has just <a href="https://www.nintendo.co.jp/support/information/2017/0929.html">announced</a> that their 'Wii Shop Channel', which launched in December 2006, will terminate January 31st, 2019. This will be gradually introduced. Starting from March 27th, 2018, users will no longer be able to add 'Wii Points' to their account but still be able to re-download already purchased 'WiiWare' and 'Virtual Console' titles.Users can also easily use the 'Wii System Transfer' to move their content from the Wii to the Wii U if both consoles are owned.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Nintendo has promised to refund any unused 'Wii Points', 'Wii Point Prepaid Cards' and 'Nintendo Point Prepaid Cards' but it's currently unconfirmed how. We'll let you know the moment they do.</p>
