EA and Respawn Entertainment have officially revealed Titanfall 2 at the EA Play Press Conference at E3 today.<br><br>Titanfall 2 boasts six new mechs with unique abilities, all new player abilities such as the grappling hook and anti-gravity boosters, a deep progression system and an entirely new offline single player campaign.<br><br>There will be a multiplayer stress test for Titanfall 2 in the coming months so stayed tuned to find out exactly when that will be available.<br><br>Titanfall 2 will be launching on Xbox One, PC and PS4 on October 28th 2016.<br>
