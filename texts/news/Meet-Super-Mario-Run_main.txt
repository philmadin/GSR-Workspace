<br>Ahead of its launch on iPhone and iPad this December, Nintendo has released a new introductory trailer for <i>Super Mario Run</i>, as well as some new details.<br><br><br>First, there won't be any microtransactions. Though the title will be free to start, the full package will cost US$10. Mario creator Shigeru Miyamoto has been adamant that they wanted to create a mobile game that represents a quality product, and not one that's stuffed with ads or continued exploitative payments.<br><br><br>Additionally, the game will launch with 24 levels, will have a Mushroom Kingdom-builder mode, and have a playable Luigi, Yoshi and Toad (the latter being a My Nintendo award).<br><br>
