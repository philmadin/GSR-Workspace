Celebrating the release of <i>Call of Duty: WWII</i>, Sony has announced that they'll be releasing a special limited-edition PS4 console to coincide. The 1TB console, which features a military camouflage design, will be available as a bundled purchase with the game. A matching green DualShock 4 controller will also be included. <i>Call of Duty: WWII</i> will contain bonus digital content such as the 'Divisions Pack', 'Nazi Zombie Pack' and a 'Weapon Unlock Token'.<p><br><p><br></p><p>For those who didn't want their PS4 blending into their home entertainment unit, the bundle is also available with a black console. For a fraction of the price though, the game will not include any DLCs.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The date of release is November 3rd 2017, but is now available for preorder.</p><p></p></p>
