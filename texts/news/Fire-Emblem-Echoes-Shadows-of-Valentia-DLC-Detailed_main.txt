<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150.0%">The long-running and growing strategy RPG series <i style="line-height:150.0%">Fire Emblem</i> will soon see a brand new adventure in the form of <i style="line-height:150.0%">Fire Emblem Echoes: Shadows of Valentia</i>, which launches on <a href="http://www.nintendo.com.au/nintendo-3ds-xl" target="_blank" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?hl=en&amp;q=http://www.nintendo.com.au/nintendo-3ds-xl&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1493952835079000&amp;usg=AFQjCNFYHcD3mCefnKRNYoaKpXmdwUoHgg" style="line-height:150.0%">Nintendo 3DS family systems</a> on 20th May. This reimagining of the Japan-exclusive <i style="line-height:150.0%">Fire Emblem Gaiden </i>will provide players with the story of long-lost childhood friends Alm and Celica, and their separate quests to bring peace to the land of Valentia.&nbsp; </p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150.0%">&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150.0%"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150.0%"><br></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150.0%">From 20th May, players will be able to expand the adventure further with five major downloadable content packs, allowing players to earn more gold and experience, explore the hidden past of Valentia, and recruit new playable characters.
