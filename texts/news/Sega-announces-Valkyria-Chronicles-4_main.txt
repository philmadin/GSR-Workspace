<p>Sega has just confirmed the next <i>Valkyria Chronicles</i> game, <i>Valkyria Chronicles 4</i>, which will be released worldwide for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch. Japan will not be getting an Xbox One version but anticipates a launch for the PlayStation 4 on March 21, 2018, and for the Nintendo Switch during the following summer. North America and Europe will be getting its own launch next year with dates yet to be confirmed.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Fans of the tactical RPG series can expect all-new characters, including Commander Claude Wallace, engineer/heavy weapons expert Riley Miller, hothead Darcsen Raz, sniper Kai Schulen, and more. Added to that, a new class of soldier specialising in using grenade launchers to shoot long ranges called a 'Grenadier' is also being introduced. The game will be set in the same 1935-esque timeline, similar to the previous games in a colder, harsher environment with combat modes split between turn-based strategy, RPG, and third-person shooting.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Watch the announcement trailer below!</p>
