Madden 17, the next installment in the long running Madden franchise, has been announced at the EA Play Press Conference today.<br><br>Madden 17 will provide players with an all new way to play franchise mode with a renewed focus on eSports events and competitive gaming. EA Challenger and Premier events, and a championship series has been announced for the game.<br><br>Madden 17 will be launching on PS3, Xbox 360, PS4 and Xbox One on August 23rd, 2016.<br>
