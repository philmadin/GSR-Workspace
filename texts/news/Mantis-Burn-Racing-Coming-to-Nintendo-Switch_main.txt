<p>A few days ago VooFoo Studios, a UK based indie developer and publisher, announced that their award-nominated racing game&nbsp;<i>Mantis Burn Racing</i>&nbsp;is coming to the Nintendo Switch eShop.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Mantis Burn Racing</i> is a top-down racing game that uses real-world 'fun' physics with intuitive controls. There are five unique classes of vehicles you use over an 11-season career where the hectic, bumper-to-bumper races will challenge your skills.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>It includes multiplayer for up to four people locally and eight online. It has a RPG-style upgrade system and contains 'Battle Cars', 'Elite' Class Vehicles, and 'Snowbound' tracks as additional DLC packs.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Check out the trailer below!</p>
