<p>Techland has officially unveiled the details of <i>Dying Light</i>'s The Outstanding Super-Crane event, which starts October 26th and ends November 1st.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>During the limited period, the grappling hook will have the cooldown handicap removed, allowing players to swing freely between buildings and also pull enemies in for easier finishes.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>This ties in with the challenge that the community has been given for this Halloween. If they reach a global kill target of 10,000,000 enemies - with fall damage they can attain a Bounty Skin Pack. Individual players also have the opportunity to nab a Gold Tier weapon if they catch 5,000 enemies with the grappling hook.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Players can also unlock a free new set of items at <a href="http://Gemly.com">Gemly.com</a> that include an exclusive shotgun skin, a new poison weapon, and a third item that has yet to be unveiled, all within The Nightmare Pack.</p>
