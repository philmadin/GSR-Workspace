EA has announced it's new program EA Originals today at the EA Play Press Conference. This program is designed to support small developers get there games out to a large audience with help from EA in regards to marketing and support.<br><br>The first game announced to be part of this program was Fe, an open world and open ended narrative experience where you play as a small creature in a forest learning about the connection between all of its flora and fauna whilst avoiding the ominous threat known as The Silent Ones.<br><br>No release date has been announced yet, but be sure to keep an eye out here for more details on Fe.<br>
