The Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege Skull Rain update is now available and includes a new Favela map, the most destructible environment to date. It introduces two new BOPE operators: Caveira and Capitão. Caveira is a ruthless interrogator who uses her stealth technique, the Silent Step, to sneak up on her targets undetected.
