Moira, the newest Support hero will soon be joining the <i>Overwatch</i> cast. As a scientist, she pushed the boundaries by experimenting on both herself and others. This lead her to a point where she was no longer welcomed at Overwatch.<br><br>Moira is able to both heal and damage by using a combination of her abilities which include: healing beams, teleports and a life drain. <br><br>It is hoped that Moira will fill the demand for more healing characters. As a hybrid character she will be able to defend herself when on her own. The release date for Moira is unknown at this stage. <br>
