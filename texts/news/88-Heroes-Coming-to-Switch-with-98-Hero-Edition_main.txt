<p></p><p>If you're a fan of retro platforming or even just the number eight, <i>88 Heroes</i> may just be the game for you. Developed by Bitmap Bureau and published by Rising Star Games back in March this year, <i>88 Heroes - 98 Heroes Edition</i>&nbsp;is bringing the strangest, dumbest and most pointless superheroes in the world to the Switch on October 6.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>88 Heroes</i>&nbsp;is a chaotic&nbsp;2D platformer, where each of the 88 levels must be completed in 88 seconds by (wait for it), 88 different heroes. As a homage to some of the most iconic platforming concepts we've seen over the years, the goal is to make it to Dr H8's penthouse before the 88-minute Doomsday timer runs out to save the world.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The Switch edition of the game features the 'RSG Champions' DLC pack as well as the upcoming 'H8 Mode Activate' DLC, bringing an extra 10 heroes to the game alongside extra challenge levels.&nbsp;</p><p></p>
