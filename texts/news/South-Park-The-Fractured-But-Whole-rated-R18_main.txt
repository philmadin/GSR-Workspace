<p class="MsoNormal">Ubisoft has just announced that their upcoming game <i>South Park:The Fractured But Whole</i>( will be classified R18+ in Australia. Australian gamers can breathe a collective sigh of relief since Ubisoft has also reassured fans that the game will be 100% uncut and uncensored. Despite the 2014 award-winning <i>South Park: The Stick Of Truth</i>&nbsp;also being R18+, stages and scenes were censored with an image of a crying koala in order to comply with Australia's censorship board. Gameplay videos of the upcoming game that were showcased at this year's E3 sparked rumours that the game will be severely censored in Australia due to explicit content.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Set after the events of <i>South Park: The Stick Of Truth</i> in the same familiar Colorado town, Eric Cartman teams up with his friends and you to create a new team of superheroes: Coon &amp; Friends. Reprising your role as the New Kid, you play in the same JRPG (Japanese Role Playing Game) inspired turn-based combat system to eliminate enemies and ultimately overthrow the dark power that threatens to destroy South Park.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">This news comes just days after the new South Park smartphone game was announced for pre-registration on Android and IOS. <i>SouthPark: Phone Destroyer</i>&nbsp;by the same developers Ubisoft, plays like an action packed real-time strategy game mixed in with elements of PVP and card collecting. The free-to-play game lets you play the role of the New Kid as you assemble the ultimate team of cowboys, wizards, cyborgs and more to fight in online PVP battles. Although no official release date has been posted just yet,all pre-registrations come with an exclusive ManBearPig outfit for your avatar.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">From the creators of South Park, Trey Parker and Matt Stone, and developed by Ubisoft San Francisco, South Park: The Fractured But Whole will be released on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Windows PC on October 17th.&nbsp;</p>
