<p>On October 11, get ready to battle a quarter of a mile at a time. As part of a collaboration with Universal, Dodge and Nissan, Psyonix will be releasing two new cars from the Fast &amp; Furious franchise into <i>Rocket League</i>.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Psyonix and Universal collaborated earlier this year when they released the Dodge Ice Charger DLC to celebrate the release of <i>The Fate of the Furious</i>. This latest update sees the addition of&nbsp;Dom's famous '70 Dodge Charger R/T, and Brian's '99 R34 GT-R Skyline from <i>2 Fast 2 Furious&nbsp;</i>into <i>Rocket League.</i></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Both cars come with a new set of wheels and decals and will be sold individually for the equivalent of $1.99 USD (~$2.50 AUD).</p>
