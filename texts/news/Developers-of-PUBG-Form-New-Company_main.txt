<p>Good news for <i>PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS</i> fans: Bluehole, Inc. has announced the formation of PUBG Corp. PUBG Corp. is a subsidiary that will be entirely focused on the development and global business opportunities for the hit game.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Chang Han Kim has been appointed Chief Executive Officer and Woonghee Cho will be serving as the Chief Operating Officer. Chang Han Kim led development of PUBG and previously worked at Vice President and Executive Producer for Bluehole, Inc. Woonghee Cho will be focusing on fast-tracking the business development and managing global operations for PUBG.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Chang Han Kim, CEO of PUBG Corp., has said, "Given PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS global success, we want to ensure that we have the operational efficiency that is required to support the game globally. This new structure allows us to be nimble as we look towards the expansion of strategic business opportunities that include the game's potential in the esports sector and the growth of PUBG as a true global IP franchise."</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>PUBG Corp. has also recently established an office in the United States and plans to do the same in Europe and Japan. The global expansion will grant players more centralised points of contact and ensures that PUBG's growth will continue to be global.</p>
