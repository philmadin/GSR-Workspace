2015's cult horror game <i>Yomawari: Night Alone</i> spawned a sequel for Japanese release back in August, this year. <i>Yomawari: Midnight Shadows</i>, which will finally be making its debut in the West, has uploaded a new trailer for anxiously awaiting fans.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Separated by a mysterious attacker, protagonists Haru and Yui must explore a creepy town full of evil in order to reconnect and go home. The gameplay has the player switch between the perspectives of each girl and the decisions chosen for one will impact the story of the other.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The upcoming game will be released on PlayStation 4, PS Vita and Steam on October 24th in North America, October 27th in Europe and November 3rd in Australia.
