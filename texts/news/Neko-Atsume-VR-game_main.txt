The hit mobile game <i>Neko Atsume</i> will be getting a VR game for the PlayStation as announced at the 2017 PlayStation Press Conference on Tuesday.<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Translated from Japanese, <i>Neko Atsume</i> means 'Cat Collector' and the mobile game plays the same way. The player purchases virtual food, toys and furniture in order to attract a variety of cute cats and "collect" them. There are 62 different cats to collect and the game is largely free to play (with the exception of some in-app purchases).</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The VR version is expected to release in 2018.</p>
