<p class="MsoNormal">September 21st to 24th will see the annual <a href="http://expo.nikkeibp.co.jp/tgs/2017/public/en/">Tokyo Game Show</a> open its doors once again. With an expected number of 250,000 visitors, this is one of Asia's largest gaming Expos. This year's theme is 'Reality Unlocked', a core belief that technological advancements in videogames have been able to provide innovative new experiences. In addition to gameplay and stage shows, TGS will feature a variety of new experiences such as thrilling e-Sports tournaments and live streaming to the world.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Designated exhibition areas for Smartphone games, e-Sports,VR/AR, Indie projects, Romance Simulations, Family Friendly games and a Merchandise Sales area have all been scheduled. An impressive list of exhibitors at this year's show include: Konami, Bandai Namco Entertainment,Capcom, Square Enix, Sega Games as well as 327 up and coming developers. The corresponding<a href="http://awards.cesa.or.jp/en/index.html"> 2017 Japan Game Awards</a> will also feature at the event, awarding Japanese developers in 'Games of the year', 'Future' and 'Amateur' divisions. <br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br>Attendees may cosplay as their favourite characters but long queues to test new games are to be expected. Buying tickets <a href="http://expo.nikkeibp.co.jp/tgs/2017/public/en/visitor/ticket.html">online</a> or from a Japanese convenience store in advance will help save some time and around 200 yen. Keep in mind that although the show runs for four days, the first two days aren't open to the general public, but for industry and press crowds only.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br>We hope to see you at the show!</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br>Dates:&nbsp;&nbsp; 21st Sep-24th Sep, 2017</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Start/End Time:&nbsp;&nbsp;10:00am- 5:00pm</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Venue: <a href="http://www.m-messe.co.jp/en/access/">Makuhari Messe</a>,Chiba Prefecture, JAPAN</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Tickets:&nbsp;Adults: Advance sales: 1,000 yen</p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><br></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">At the door: 1,200 yen</span><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal">Children elementary school age and under: Free entrance</p>
