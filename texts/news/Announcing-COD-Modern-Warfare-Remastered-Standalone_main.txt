<p>Activision have announced that <i>Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered</i> (<i>COD:MWR</i>) will be available as a standalone title, releasing today (27th of June, 2017). Originally included with the Legacy and Deluxe editions of&nbsp;<i>COD:</i> <i>Infinite Warfare</i>, you can now jump into one of the most critically acclaimed and genre-defining games in history on its own.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p></p><p><i>Modern Warfare Remastered</i> features fully improved texture resolution and detail, revamped animations, remastered audio, and much more. Relive the full, iconic story campaign as you're transported around the globe with Capt. Price, Gaz and Soap across all the missions from the original game, including "All Ghillied Up," "Charlie Don't Surf," and "Crew Expendable."&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Players will also experience the online multiplayer mode that redefined Call of Duty by introducing killstreaks, XP, Prestige and more, and battle in the fan-favourite maps such as "Crash," "Backlot" and "Crossfire."</p><p></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>"It's been an honour to bring this great title to a new generation of players," said Brian Raffel, studio head of Raven Software. "With <i>Modern Warfare Remastered</i>, we're able to relive our favourite campaign levels and multiplayer moments in full HD visuals and remastered audio. &nbsp;No detail was too small, as the team pored over every aspect of development."</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>In celebration of the <i>COD:MWR</i> launch, players will also be able to participate in the newest seasonal event called, Call of Duty "Days of Summer." The five-week community celebration begins June 27th and will feature a tonne of in-game giveaways, XP events and new playlists across multiple titles, including a new summer-themed map for <i>MWR</i> that will be available through the duration of the event.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>COD:MWR</i>&nbsp;releases in stores for&nbsp;PS4 and via download on the PlayStation Network, with other platforms (PC and Xbox) yet to be announced. It will be available at an RRP of AU$69.99, and the Variety Map Pack will set you back an extra AU$20.&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">Given that it is possible to get </span><i>Infinite Warfare: Legacy Edition</i><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">&nbsp;for less than AU$70, whether or not the standalone is worth it is up to you.</span></p>
