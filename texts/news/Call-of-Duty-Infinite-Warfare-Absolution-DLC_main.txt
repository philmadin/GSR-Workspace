<p>Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare Absolution, is available now on PlayStation 4. Published by Activision and developed by Infinity Ward, Absolution features four new, diverse multiplayer maps that are designed to showcase the different play styles experienced within the game. The DLC pack also includes &nbsp;"Attack of the &nbsp;Radioactive &nbsp;Thing!" with actress Cassandra Peterson starring as Elvira in a 1950's Creature Feature zombies experience.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Attack of the Radioactive Thing!, is the next chapter of the Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare Zombies story, which features the return of Willard Wyler, the villain of this story. Players will fight against a new array of zombie enemies with new weapons, traps and more.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>In addition to this, Absolution will include four new multiplayer maps: Bermuda, Permafrost, Fore and Ember. These maps cater to all playstyles, whether is be close quarters or long range, it is meant to be fun for all.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>The DLC Map Pack is available via the Season Pass or with the purchase of the Legacy Pro or Digital Deluxe editions of the game.</p>
