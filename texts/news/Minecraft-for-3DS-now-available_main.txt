<p>Nintendo just launched <i>Minecraft</i> for the Nintendo 3DS with less than a day's notice. The surprise announcement was made through one of its Nintendo Direct live streams. Although both digital and physical versions of the game were confirmed for the 3DS, the physical cartridge edition won't be available until a later date.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><i>Minecraft</i> will come unchanged from the original but will be packed full of features such as five skin packs, two texture packs, a survival mode and a creative mode. The game will also utilise the dual screens of the console by letting you check your map and manage your inventory through the touch screen.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>Mine your way into the Nintendo 3DS eShop for a copy.</p>
