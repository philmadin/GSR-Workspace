<p>There really isn't much more to say about the audio that wasn't said in the last review. Once again, the sound effects are abundant and beautiful. They were used effectively alongside the soundtrack, which also held up to the standard set by its predecessor.</p><p>I had a near-religious experience during one of the new missions as the music swelled, rose, and fell at the right moments to build suspense or release tension. It was one of the better examples I've seen of incidental music used to add to the emotional investment I had in the game.</p>
