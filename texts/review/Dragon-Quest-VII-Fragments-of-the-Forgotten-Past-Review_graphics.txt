<br>Akira Toriyama's trademark style has always ensured that <i>Dragon Quest</i> looks great, no matter what system it runs on.<br><br><br>It's no different here, with the modest 3DS specs no barrier to a great looking game. The anime aesthetic is perfect, with bright colours that pop, and exaggerated character animations that is very Nintendo-esque in its execution.<br><br><br>The 2D sprites from the 2001 version have been replaced with full 3D models, and scenery has been given a fresh lick of paint. Sometimes, though, things can look a little basic, like the smudgy overworld or limited NPC variety, but it's nothing too distracting. <br>
