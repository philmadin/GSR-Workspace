<br>The story is set up through still cut-scenes and a voice over that informs the player that Darius just wants some sleep, yet can't start counting sheep until the party-goers next door stop the loud music. Like any sensible person, he is eventually fed up and begins to clear the house of all guests but instead of calling the police, he takes a knife and kills them all. Darius is basically the definition of a mood killer. <p><br></p><p>The game leads you through different locations with increasingly more guests, but never strays far from the initial story setup, leaving your endless endeavour to rid the world of techno music quite empty. </p>
