Naughty Dog manages to blend cinematic flair and creative designs to make sure that <i>Uncharted 4</i> hooks you right to the end. The game has toned down on gunfights in order to focus on delivering a strong story. <i>Uncharted 4</i> is a visual benchmark for future games with its highly detailed cutscenes and environments. Nathan Drake's adventure may be over but it wont be forgotten quickly.
