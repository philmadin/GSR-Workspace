<br>The opportunities presented in the game so far have been creative, relatively original, and varied. But in this episode, three of them revolve around a secret tunnel connecting the consulate to the marketplace. <p><br></p><p>Yet another opportunity is as simple as “collect this key”. The others are mildly more interesting, but are nothing compared to those in Sapienza or Paris, which are all in different locations and have varied gameplay.<br><br><br>On the plus side, between the sheer number of people on the map, and the doors that are either locked or closely guarded, it’s much harder to find somewhere quiet in Marrakech than in Paris or Sapienza. This requires the player to think differently and be slightly more creative, which is exactly what one wants from a <i>Hitman</i> game (unless guns blazing is your thing).</p>
