<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:10.5pt;line-height:107%;font-family:" arial",sans-serif"="">In a constant state of updates, <i style="font-size:10.5pt;line-height:107%;font-family:">Golf With Your Friends </i>is continuously releasing more content, with each patch more exciting than the last. Currently, up to 12 players can compete across the three various maps, each containing 18 holes. Using your mouse to control your ball, you can opt for regular putt-putt, or choose to enable a myriad of modifications that affects factors such as gravity, ball type, collision, jumping and more. A few bugs regarding not spawning after falling off the course hinder the overall experience slightly, but this will likely be fixed with time. <o:p style="font-size:10.5pt;line-height:107%;font-family:"></o:p></span></p>
