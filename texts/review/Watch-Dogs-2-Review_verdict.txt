<i><br>Watch_Dogs 2 </i>is the <i>Assassins Creed 2</i> of this new franchise, taking what worked well with the first and expanding on it in every way. Hacking feels fresher, the action handles well and the overall tone of the game is campy hacker heaven. In the need of a new open world game? <i>Watch_Dogs 2</i> is the answer.
