<i>Mankind Divided</i> is everything a successful sequel needs to be. It enhances the original product in almost every way while still offering a multitude of new features, it furthers the incredible plot of the <i>Deus Ex</i> universe and ultimately is just a fun game in every way imaginable.&nbsp;<p><i>Deus Ex Mankind Divided</i> is a GOTY contender, and the very best game in a genre it has defined all on its own.</p>
