Developers Fatshark have teamed up with Games Workshop to bring us arguably the most thrilling and challenging reproduction of the Warhammer universe in video game form. Warhammer: End Times – Vermintide is a co-op first person shooter that allows players to team up in groups of four in a ‘Left 4 Dead’-esque fashion that focuses on brutal combat sequences, objective based levels and strong team play, as waves of Skaven attempt to thwart you at every turn.
