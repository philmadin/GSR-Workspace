Visuals are very disappointing; with basic character models and plain environments that nevertheless suffer from an inconsistent framerate – though they aren’t the worst graphics either. <br />
<br />
This is likely the fault of the game’s troubled production, as it had switched engines three times, not to mention the move to the complex Wii U architecture. Still, the cinematic scenes are agreeable, and the art can be surprisingly exceptional (especially in one particular level).
