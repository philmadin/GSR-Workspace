The graphics are never usually an issue in an EA Sports title and this is no exception. The fighters look fantastic and nearly picture perfect when shown next to their real life counterparts.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p>UFC 2 brings the introduction of hair physics as well as a new body deformation system for when strikes are absorbed. The bruising, cuts, lumps and sweat dripping from brows is at its absolute finest and five round wars can often result in two completely different looking fighters than those who entered the octagon.</p>
