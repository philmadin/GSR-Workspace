<p>The gameplay in <i>Trials and Tribulations</i> is the same as in the second Ace Attorney game, <i>Justice for All</i>. You have your investigation segments where you talk to various people and gather evidence. During the investigation, you also have parts where you use your ‘Magatama’ to discover secrets people are keeping.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>You use all the information and evidence you gathered in the investigation segments to use in the Court segments. In this, you Cross Examine witnesses, pressing them for more information or presenting evidence that contradicts their statements. The decisions that made here will decide whether or not the defendant is declared ‘Not Guilty’ or not.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p>
