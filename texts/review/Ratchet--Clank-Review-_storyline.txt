This is not your average retelling. <i>Insomniac Games</i> have approached this as a new experience for <i>Ratchet & Clank</i>. The game tells the tale of Ratchet, an enthusiastic young Lombax, with hopes of joining the Galactic Rangers. After rescuing Clank, a defective warbot, from a crashed spacecraft and meeting up with Captain Qwark, Ratchet finds out that there's a much larger threat to the galaxy. <p align="LEFT" style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration: none; margin-bottom: 0cm;">The story for <i style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration: none; margin-bottom: 0cm;">Ratchet & Clank</i> expands upon the original game, delving into more detail than ever before. Ratchet has been given more dimension and character with additional back story to make him more relatable. The game maintains it's light-hearted story with plenty of jokes and witty narration. This is balanced with hectic, action-filled sequences to create a memorable re-imagining.</p>
