From the menu screen to the opening cinematic to the final credits. <i>Deus Ex</i> has continued its improvements by creating dark beats and sinister tones that provide a layer of menacing mystery behind crucial in game moments.&nbsp;<p>Twists in the plot are accompanied by intelligent music choices that help make every moment have more impact. The voice acting is of a very high standard and includes a wide array of diverse nationalities such as Australian, Czechoslovakian, American, British and Russian. The ability to have such well done voice overs for such unique countries is a complement to the studio.</p>
