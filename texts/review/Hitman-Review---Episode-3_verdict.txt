<br>It’s easy to say that Episode 3 of <i>Hitman</i> is the lowest point of the series. It’s not as much fun, the colours aren’t as pretty, and it seems smaller than other maps - at least in terms of interesting areas. But it’s just as easy to overlook all that Marrakech has to offer, and it’s clear that IO-Interactive’s standards are still as high as they’ve ever been.&nbsp;<p><br></p><p><br></p><p>If you enjoyed other episodes, this still offers the full <i>Hitman</i> experience you love, and still deserves your attention.</p>
