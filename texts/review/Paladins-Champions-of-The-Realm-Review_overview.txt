&lt;i&gt;Paladins: Champions of The Realm&lt;/i&gt; is a first person multiplayer team shooter currently in open Beta. It is developed and published by Hi-Rez Studios, creators of the &lt;i&gt;SMITE&lt;/i&gt; series. The open beta has been available for PC since September 2016and has now been made available for users of the PS4 and Xbox one consoles as of the 3rd of May 2017.&lt;br&gt;
