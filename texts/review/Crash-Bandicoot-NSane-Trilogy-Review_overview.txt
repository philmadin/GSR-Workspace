Entire console generations have come and gone without one of the video games most iconic and lovable characters. In 2017 we have now witnessed the comeback of the poster marsupial of the early PlayStation era - the high-octane bandicoot simply known as Crash. Vicarious Visions have lovingly crafted one of the best remasters of our time and reminded us of just how much we missed the charm of &lt;i&gt;Crash Bandicoot&lt;/i&gt;.
