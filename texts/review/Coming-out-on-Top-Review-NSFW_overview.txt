Coming out on Top (CooT) is a visual novel/dating sim focused on Mark Matthews, a young man in his final year of College. Having just come out as gay to his friends, Mark enters the world of dating apps, gay bars, awkward dates and erotic adventures.<br />
<br />
The game features heavy sexual content in the form of still images that show absolutely everything and frequent references to sex throughout the game so it’s fair to say this game isn’t for everyone.
