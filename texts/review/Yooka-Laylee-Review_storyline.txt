&lt;i&gt;Yooka-Laylee&lt;/i&gt; follows the adventures of the titular duo Yooka the chameleon and Laylee the bat.&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;The pair have just renovated their home, when suddenly the villainous Capital Bee and Dr Quack turn on their book vacuum in the hopes of capturing a very special magical book. As the book is being sucked away, the Pagies all fall out and are spread across all of the many book worlds. After this, Yooka and Laylee set out to retrieve all of the Pagies and stop Capital Bee and Dr Quack from fulfilling their evil plan.&amp;nbsp; &lt;br&gt;
