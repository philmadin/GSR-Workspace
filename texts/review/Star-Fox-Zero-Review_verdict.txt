In many ways, Star Fox Zero is an incredibly conservative game, yet it also displays an unabashed desire to throw expectations out the window. Honestly, I cannot comment on whether it fails or succeeds. It will go down in history as one of those 'love it or loathe it' type of games, but if I must proclaim anything, then <p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">I will say that I'm itching for another go – and that's not something I say very often!</span><br></p>
