&lt;i&gt;Mistik&lt;/i&gt; the DJ - or the character you play as in the game - gets stranded in the desert when his motorbike crashes and breaks down. You must use your sound recorder to capture sounds from objects, other beings and abstract elements in this surrealistic world to create some sick beats.
