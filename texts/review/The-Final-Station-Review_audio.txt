<i><br>The Final Station </i>has an incredible soundtrack, atmospheric and emotional, perfectly capturing the world you inhabit. It is equal parts spooky and beautiful, highlighting environments and situations almost perfectly.<br><br><br>The sound design is also excellent featuring many of the small details that similar games may overlook. From feet crunching on snow to ringing ears when a gun is fired in an enclosed space, it all sounds great. <br><br><br>The audio adds so much to the games atmosphere that without it you would most certainly have a lesser title.<br>
