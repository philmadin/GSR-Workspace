<br>The sequel to the JRPG sleeper hit, <i>The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel</i>, <i>Trails of Cold Steel II</i> is a fantasy/steampunk adventure that contains a deep political espionage plot, and an equally involving gameplay system. Initially starting the game with a complete lack of knowledge, I was incredibly surprised to discover one of the best games of 2016!<br>
