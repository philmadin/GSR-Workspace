<p class="MsoNormal">The <i>Horizon</i> games have a distinct feel that separate it amongst the crowd of open-world racers. The cars all handle differently which lends to the nature of the sim racing roots while giving you the freedom to launch off ramps and drift around the lush rain forest location in game. The arcadey system mixed with the great <i>Forza</i> handling will have you feeling like a racing god, with options to increase difficulty if you want an extra challenge.</p><p class="MsoNormal"><br></p><p class="MsoNormal"><o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal">The feeling of driving down the local Surfers Paradise streets is unlike any racing game yet and a certain joy comes with playing a <i>Forza</i> game that I’m yet to match. The game captures the fast pace action of circuit racing perfectly while allowing you to cruise down the Australian coastline at any pace you’d like, just watch out for all the other hoons.<o:p></o:p></p>
