<i>Firewatch</i> delivers some of the best interactions between two characters you may ever see in a modern video game.<br><br>For the entirety of the game, you primarily interact with Delilah through the radio. Depending on your responses Delilah’s replies can range from kind, snarky, sweet, comedic and flirtatious.<br><br>It’s almost like reading a book and imagining what a character looks like in your head but never knowing for sure. The banter between 'Hank' and 'D' is simply priceless.<br>
