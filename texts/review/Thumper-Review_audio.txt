<i><br>Thumper </i>is nothing without it's audio. The music, a deep rhythmic beat, dark and intense, and the sound of the thumper grinding it's way along the track, combine to create a unique and interesting sound.<br><br><br>It's really great and has definitely earned a separate soundtrack on the steam store though it's much more pleasurable to listen to in-game as your actions align with the beats of the music, adding depth to an already great score.<br>
