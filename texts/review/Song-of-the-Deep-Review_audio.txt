From the moment <i>Song of the Deep</i> begins, the restful, tranquil score takes the lead. Everywhere you go there’s a wonderful or eerie tune to follow. Perhaps it doesn’t energise the gameplay, being so comforting, but it works and definitely lifts the experience.<p><br></p><p>The muffling of sound underwater is an effective little side note of the experience. Rapid movement, torpedoes, are distorted and with no real sound to speak of outside of these, the void can be filled with the aforementioned music.</p>
