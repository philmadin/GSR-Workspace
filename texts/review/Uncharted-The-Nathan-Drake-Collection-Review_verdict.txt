More trophies and challenges are always welcomed for a series like this. The replay value of Uncharted is extended with new difficulties and a 'Speed Run' mode for the more serious players. Photo mode allows the player to share their best pictures of the game with friends online. <br />
<br />
The Uncharted: Nathan Drake Collection is a worthwhile addition for any gamer's library. The progression and story of Nathan Drake as a character and game icon is impressive as it has inspired various games in its wake. Bluepoint Studios have outdone themselves in updating visuals and controls with plenty to do after completion.<br />
