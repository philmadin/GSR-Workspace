<br><i>SUPERHOT</i> does have a storyline, if an incredibly thin one. A friend informs you of a new crack to the program superhot.exe. As you play things happen causing you to question reality and face the truth.<br><br>Unfortunately the basis of the story is never really expanded upon, a textbook case of wasted potential. It could have been elevated to a higher level but feels as if it was sidelined over the gameplay. <br><br>
