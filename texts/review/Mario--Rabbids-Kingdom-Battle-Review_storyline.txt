Thanks to the mischievous Rabbids, the Mushroom Kingdom is in complete disarray. After stealing a prototype &#039;SupaMerge&#039; helmet, they inadvertently open an interdimensional vortex between the real world and the Kingdom, merging with a bunch of Mario paraphernalia and real world objects along the way. Your job is to defeat the manic Rabbids and their absurd creations, bringing peace back to the Mushroom Kingdom.&amp;nbsp;
