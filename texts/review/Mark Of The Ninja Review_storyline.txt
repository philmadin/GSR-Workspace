After being awakened by a ninja named Ora, the protagonist is confronted with an attack on his ancient ninja clan by heavily-armed modern soldiers. Using a variety of ninja tools and the strange powers granted by a mysterious ink, the protagonist and Ora are sent to chase down and destroy the corporation behind the attack before discovering the real reason they were given this mission...<br />
<br />
A plot inspired by classic ninja concepts meets the modern world in a thrilling story that doesn't ask for attention until the end of the game, where a beautiful flashback sequence recaps it in case you missed it. Mark Of The Ninja starts on a high note and never lets go, with memorable bombshells and plot twists throughout.<br />
<br />
Mark Of The Ninja's story is everything a ninja story needs, played to great effect.
