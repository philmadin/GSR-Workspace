<i>Ratchet & Clank</i> is a well rounded game that has few flaws. Having in game collectables like Holocards and Gold Bolts, plus trophies,gives players many things to achieve over multiple runs. The audio and visuals are amazing while the gameplay is action packed and often amusing. <i>Insomniac Games</i> have truly pushed themselves to present a fun game with plenty of replay value.
